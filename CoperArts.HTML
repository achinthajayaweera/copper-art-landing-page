✦ Our Story

Where History
Finds a Home

Copper Arts Antiques began with a simple belief: that objects of historical significance deserve to be cherished, not forgotten. Nestled in Battaramulla, we have spent years building relationships with collectors, estates, and heritage custodians across Sri Lanka.

Our collection spans centuries and cultures — from ancient bronze ritual vessels to Dutch colonial furniture, from Kandyan-era jewellery to Buddhist ceremonial objects. Each piece is carefully authenticated, documented, and presented with the reverence it deserves.

"An antique is not merely old — it is a conversation with the past."
